Dr. Mamoloto Tlabela

PhD, SAIEE, Former Acting Head of Electricity City of Tshwane

With over 35 years of electrical engineering experience, Dr. Tlabela is a respected thought leader in power systems, smart grid, and energy infrastructure. He has held senior roles in municipal electricity, academic research, and international innovation programmes.

He is the founder of Mamoloto Technologies and the architect of the Solar PV 4.0 framework. He is also an author, conference speaker, and active contributor to engineering standards in Africa.
