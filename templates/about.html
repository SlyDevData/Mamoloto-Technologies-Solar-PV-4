About Us

Mamoloto Technologies is a South African EPC company dedicated to solar PV and renewable energy solutions.

Building a Sustainable South Africa

Founded in 2018 by Dr. Mamoloto Tlabela, Mamoloto Technologies (PTY) LTD was established to address critical challenges in South Africa's electricity sector — copper theft, ongoing load-shedding, and environmental damage from fossil fuels.

With over 34 years of electrical engineering experience, Dr. Tlabela leads the company with a vision to harness renewable energy for a safer, cleaner future.

Our Founder

Dr. Mamoloto Tlabela, Ph.D

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Dr. Tlabela holds a PhD in Commerce and Administration and has over 34 years of electrical engineering experience, working through the ranks from an apprentice electrician to a Regional Director.

"As the adage goes, 'every generation borrows the planet from the next', so we must ensure that the environment is sustainable for the future."

He founded Mamoloto Technologies in 2018 to manufacture, install, and upgrade solar PV systems, with plans to diversify into wind, ocean waves, and green hydrogen energy.
